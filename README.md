# Watsapp-Buisness-Agent
This is a multiagent workflow that categorizes the coming message into positive, negative and neutral. Positive message is genrated into  facebook post by AI agent. Negative message are handled by complain handling agent and neutral messages are responded by customer service agent that fetches the information from the provided knowledge base
This is not just another automation tool — it’s a smart multi-agent workflow designed specifically for small businesses that operate heavily through WhatsApp. It handles customer interactions intelligently, saving time, increasing engagement, and improving satisfaction — all automatically.

Here’s what it does:

1️⃣ Sentiment Detection – Every incoming WhatsApp message is analyzed to determine if it’s positive, negative, or neutral.

2️⃣ Intelligent Routing:

Positive messages: Automatically converted into eye-catching social media posts (with images) for Facebook and other platforms — boosting your brand presence.

Neutral queries: Instantly answered by an AI agent pulling accurate information from your business knowledge base — FAQs, product info, return policies, you name it.

Negative messages: Complaints are addressed patiently by an AI agent, who gathers all necessary details and alerts the business owner via email for immediate attention.

✅ Before any social media post goes live, the business owner approves it, ensuring full control and brand safety.

Why businesses need this:

Respond to customers instantly and accurately

Convert positive feedback into marketing opportunities

Handle complaints professionally to maintain customer trust

Automate repetitive tasks while keeping the human touch where it matters
